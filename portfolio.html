ABOUT ME

The person behind the work
YOUR PHOTO

"I don't just model characters — I give them a history, a posture, a reason to exist in the world."

I'm Alex Morgan, a 3D artist and character designer with 6+ years of professional experience across games, animation, and concept art. I studied at the École des Gobelins in Paris and have since worked with indie studios, AAA pipelines, and my own creative projects.

My work sits at the intersection of strong storytelling and technical craft. I care as much about why a character looks a certain way as I do about clean topology and efficient UV maps. To me, the best 3D character isn't the most detailed — it's the most believable.

Outside the studio I sketch constantly, obsess over creature design from nature, and play too many RPGs for research purposes.
